The CircuitLab

Explore Electrical Engineering — No Experience Needed

At The CircuitLab, we believe every student deserves the chance to explore STEM — regardless of background, resources, or prior experience. Through hands-on workshops, engaging videos, and a collaborative online community, we make electrical engineering approachable for learners from elementary school through high school.

Build a Simple LED Flashlight

Learn the fundamentals of circuits by building your own LED flashlight from scratch. You will work with resistors, LEDs, batteries, and switches — and walk away with a working device you made yourself.
